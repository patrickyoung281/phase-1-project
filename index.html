Welcome to Herr Young's German Course!

In this module, you will be creating flashcards to express how you like spending your free time.

Directions: Think of how you like to spend your free time.

  • In box 1, enter a German verb in the infinitive. This verb should be something you enjoy, z.B. schwimmen.
  • In box 2, enter the English translation of that verb in the infinitive, z.B to swim.. (Don't forget that to is part of the infinitve in English!)
  • In box 3, enter the URL of a picture representing the activity you enjoy.
